About us

GIVA's journey

In 2019, GIVA came to life, founded by three dynamic young leaders: Ishendra Agarwal, Nikita Prasad, and Sachin Shetty. Today, we proudly stand as one of India's fastest-growing direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands. GIVA's expertise lies in crafting authentic 925 fine silver jewellery, ensuring that each piece embodies the timeless allure of true craftsmanship.
